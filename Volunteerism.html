BATC Business Curriculm for Start Ups

I had the opportunity to develop curriculum for BATC for one of my classes in Instructional Technology and Learning Sciences. At the end of the course, I realized that we had only developed a small module and the rest of the business module was going to be left unfinished. I reached out to Mason Lefler, who we had worked with orginially, and I asked if I could volunteed to finish out the all of the business course. He was happy to all me to do so. It has been a tremendous opportunity for me to learn, but more importantly for me to give back to BATC.
